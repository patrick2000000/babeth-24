Departments at CPEC Saint Babeth

CPEC Saint Babeth offers technical and practical education for students in Ordinary Level (S1, S2, S3) and advanced skills training in ICT fields.

Software Development

Students learn programming and digital solutions for real-world problems.

What we learn:

  • S1 – Introduction to ICT
  • S2 – Programming Basics
  • S3 – Web Development & Databases

Multimedia

Creativity and digital content production using modern tools.

What we learn:

  • L3 – Graphic Design Basics
  • L4 – Video Editing & Animation
  • L5 – Advanced Multimedia Projects
